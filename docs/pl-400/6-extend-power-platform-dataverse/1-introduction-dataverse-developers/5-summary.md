# Summary

Microsoft Dataverse is a highly evolved platform for building robust business applications. It serves as the platform for model-driven apps and first-party apps such as Dynamics 365 Sales/Customer Service and provides extensible methods for automating business processes and advanced analytics, leveraging tools such as Power Automate and Power BI.
