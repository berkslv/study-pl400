# Summary

- 3 minutes

Working with relational data is a cornerstone for crafting efficient and scalable apps.

## Key Takeaways

- **Scalability**: Using relational data within your Power Apps enables the creation of solutions that scale effectively as your app grows.

- **Functions for Relationships**: Functions like `Filter` and `LookUp` serve as vital tools in establishing connections between parent and child tables, facilitating a structured approach to relational data handling.

- **Dataverse Simplifies Relationships**: Microsoft Dataverse offers built-in functionalities that streamline the creation of relationships between tables. The dot (.) notation simplifies accessing and expanding related data, enhancing ease and efficiency when working with relational data in your apps.
