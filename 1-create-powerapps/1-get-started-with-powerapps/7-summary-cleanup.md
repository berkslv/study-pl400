# Summary

- 2 minutes

Welcome to your journey as a Power Apps developer!

In this module, you explored what Microsoft Power Apps can do for your business, the types of data your apps can use, and how it integrates with Power Automate and Power BI.

You created your first apps by using an Excel table saved to OneDrive and explored how Copilot can help building an app.

You also learned about important design considerations when creating an app.

Continue with the next module to expand your knowledge of Power Apps.
