# Summary

This module explored how you can evaluate and improve app load times. By regularly reviewing and measuring your app, you can proactively identify and remediate startup performance problems.

## Key takeaways

- **Microsoft Azure Monitor** can help you identify formulas that are causing the app to slow down.
- **App settings** - Keeping your app updated with current app setting recommendations can help ensure that you get the best performance.
- **Connectors** - By configuring only the required connectors, you can avoid users signing in to connectors that aren't in use.
- **Data load strategy** - By configuring a data load strategy, you can ensure that you balance the need to defer loading data with the performance of having it preloaded on startup.
- **Visual feedback** - Working to make your app load as quickly as possible and providing visual feedback when appropriate can help increase user satisfaction with your app.
